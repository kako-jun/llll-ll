Welcome to Planning Division 42 — the personal site where kako-jun keeps the games and apps he has made (look for the "llll-ll" wordmark).

## About this site

Famicom-era nostalgia meets a dark, cyberpunk mood. The whole thing wears a terminal (btop) look, with small bits of playfulness scattered around — try poking the Tetris up in the header, or flip the theme between light and dark.

## How to enjoy it

- **apps** — a list of the games and apps I've built. Filter by tag or search, and try anything that catches your eye.
- **art of today** — a single picture that changes every day. Tomorrow it's a different one, so drop by now and then.
- **blog** — the stories behind what I make, and whatever I'm thinking about. I'll be moving older posts here little by little.

## About kako-jun

I'm in Kanazawa, blending nostalgia with modern tech to make games and apps. My motto is "ship it, then polish" rather than "polish it, then ship."

Take a look around — and come back sometime.
